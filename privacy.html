Your Privacy
Matters Here

A transparent look at how AhmedShehata.com handles your data — what we collect, why we collect it, and how it's kept safe.

Last updated · 2025
01
Data Collection
What Information We Gather
A — Passive Collection

Certain technical data is gathered automatically as you navigate the site — no action needed on your end. This includes:

  • Your device type and browser version
  • General location data (country or city level)
  • Pages viewed and time spent on each
  • How you arrived — via search, social media, or direct link

This is handled through Google Analytics, which processes data anonymously.

B — Active Collection

When you reach out through a contact form, we may receive:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Any details you choose to share in your message

Sharing this information is entirely your choice.

02
Data Usage
How We Put It to Use

The information collected serves a few clear purposes:

  • Enhancing the website's content and overall experience
  • Replying to your messages and questions
  • Understanding site traffic patterns and performance
  • Keeping the website secure and stable

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for any commercial or marketing purpose — ever.

03
Cookies
Small Files, Clear Purpose

This site uses cookies — lightweight files stored in your browser — to:

  • Keep certain features working smoothly
  • Gather anonymous traffic analytics
  • Remember your browsing preferences

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings.

04
External Services
Third-Party Integrations

To deliver a better experience, we rely on select external tools:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic insights
  • Google AdSense — contextual advertising
  • Contact & email delivery services

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy, which governs how they handle any data they receive.

05
Security
Keeping Your Data Safe

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect any information held on this site.

No system connected to the internet can offer a total guarantee of security. While we do our best, please be mindful when sharing sensitive information online.

06
Your Rights
You're Always in Control

You hold the following rights regarding your data:

  • Request a summary of what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to remove any details you've submitted
  • Opt out of analytics tracking via your browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, head to the Contact page and send us a message.

07
Policy Updates
When Things Change

This policy may be revised from time to time to reflect changes in how we operate or to meet legal requirements. Whenever that happens, the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be refreshed accordingly.

08
Questions?
We're Happy to Clarify

If anything in this policy is unclear, or if you'd simply like more information about how your data is handled, please don't hesitate to reach out through the Contact page.
